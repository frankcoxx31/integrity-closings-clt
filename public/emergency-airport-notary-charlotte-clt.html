Frequently Asked Questions

Questions About CLT Emergency Notary Service

Can you meet me inside the airport?
We can meet you in any publicly accessible area of Charlotte Douglas International Airport — the main terminal, check-in areas, or the ticketing level. We do not have security clearance to access the airside gate areas past the TSA checkpoint. If you've already cleared security, you'll need to exit and re-enter, or we'll need to coordinate a meeting point before you go through. Call us as early as possible so we can work out the logistics.
What ID do I need?
You'll need a valid, unexpired government-issued photo ID. A driver's license, state ID card, U.S. passport, or military ID all work. The name on your ID must match the name on the document being notarized. If you have an unusual situation — a recently changed name, an expired ID, or a foreign passport — call us ahead of time so we can confirm whether we can proceed.
Can you notarize documents late at night?
Yes, after-hours appointments are available. Late-night service carries an additional fee, and availability depends on scheduling. Call 980-372-4103 to confirm whether we can accommodate your specific time. We do our best to be available when travelers need us, including early morning departures and late arrivals.
What if my flight is delayed?
Let us know as soon as you hear about a delay. If we're already en route, we may need to adjust the meeting point or timing. In most cases we can accommodate schedule changes, especially if the delay buys us more time rather than less. Communication is key — keep us updated and we'll work with you.
Should I sign the document before you arrive?
No. Do not sign the document before we arrive. The entire point of notarization is that you sign the document in the presence of the notary so we can verify your identity and witness the signature. If you sign beforehand, we cannot notarize it and the appointment will need to be rescheduled or the document reprinted. Bring the document unsigned.
Can you print or scan documents too?
We do not carry a printer or scanner on mobile appointments. If you need a document printed before your appointment, we recommend using a FedEx or UPS Store near the airport — several are located within a few miles of CLT. If you can email the document to yourself, most hotel business centers can print it for a small fee. Let us know when you call if printing is a concern and we'll help you find an option near your location.
