When your client can't come to you — we go to them. Same-day · After-Hours · Hospital · Nursing Home · Home Visits

Integrity Closings CLT provides professional mobile notarization services for estate planning attorneys whose clients are unable to travel to the office. Whether your client is hospitalized, in a nursing home, or simply homebound, we provide same-day and after-hours service throughout the Charlotte metro area — reliably, professionally, and with the sensitivity these situations require.
